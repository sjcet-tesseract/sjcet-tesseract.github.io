RaspberryPi Workshop

The Raspberry Pi is a low cost, credit-card sized computer this little device enables people of all ages to explore computing, and to learn how to program in languages like Scratch and Python. It’s capable of doing everything you’d expect a desktop computer to do, from browsing the internet and playing high-definition video, to making spreadsheets, word-processing, and playing games. Master the Raspberry Pi platform after attending this workshop.
